THE FEMALE KITCHEN

Women have traditionally played a big role in all parts of food, from gathering to production to cooking and consumption. Having been inspired by the incredible female chefs, who on a daily basis run kitchens around the world, we decided to build this website showcasing their abilities and achievements. Here you will find the profile of 15 inspiring female chefs from around the globe and links to their own personal recipes. Besides the profiles, you can also find a brief history of women and cooking, and the main adversity which they face in the kitchen nowadays: sexism. To learn more, please explore the website.
