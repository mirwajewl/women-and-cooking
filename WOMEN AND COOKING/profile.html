Asma Khan is an Indian-born chef, and cookbook author. She is the owner of Darjeeling Express restaurant in Soho and was profiled on the sixth season of the documentary series Chef's Table. In June 2019 Business Insider named her number 1 on their list of "100 Coolest People in Food and Drink"

Before opening her restaurant, Asma used to organise a supper club in her home in 2012. In 2015, she opened a pop-up in Soho to much acclaim, and Darjeeling Express the restaurant opened its doors in June 2017 in London.

Second daughter fund:

In contrast to boys, who are widely celebrated, and first daughters who are also celebrated but to a lesser extent, second daughters are mourned. To combat this tradition which can have life long effects on these girls, Asma has created a charity which donates them celebration packages.

Nigella Lawson is a journalist, television personality and food writer. She started to work as a book reviewer and restaurant critic, and then began her career as a freelance journalist, writing for a number of newspapers and magazines. Her dishes are known for their simplicity and do not require an elaborate preparation.


Recipe: Totally chocolate chocolate chip cookies

Ania Starmach is a Polish chef, cookbook author, and celerity chef. She has gained recognition by being a juror of the Polish edition of the MasterChef and MasterChef Junior. Ania completed her training in renowned restaurants, including in the French Lameloise, Krakow's Ancora and restaurants at the Old Hotel.


Recipe: Placki z jabłkami – racuchy

Siri Barje is a Swedish chef and food blogger. She trained as a chef at Grythyttan and has over the years worked at restaurants such as Esperanto, Shibumi and Sally Voltaire & Sisters. Nowadays, she is a regular guest chef on TV4's News Morning, and blogs extensively on köket.se, where she shares a variety of dishes on her Instagram account Olive Lobster.


Recipe: Vegan Fried Rice

Olia Hercules was born in the south of Ukraine. After finishing school, she moved to the UK where she studied Italian language and International Relations at the University of Warwick. After spending a year in Italy, Olia settled in London, pursuing a journalistic career after completing her Master’s degree.


Following the financial crisis of 2008, Olia decided to quit her job as a film business reporter to pursue her dream to cook for a living.


Recipe: Green Beans Dumplings

Sikke Sumari is a Finnish self-taught restaurateur and cook. Sumari has worked for television since 1975, first as an announcer on MTV nd as a presenter of entertainment and news programs, including Tasaraha, Usko or ne, Dolce Vita, and Kotikeittiömestari. She also hosted the program Kokkisota from 1999–2004 and continued as a presenter in 2017. She has been a restaurateur since 1996, when she and her husband, Tony Ilmon, founded Tony's Deli.


Recipe: Artichoke Soup

Yewande Komolafe is a recipe developer, food stylist and author of the New York Times “10 Essential Nigerian Recipes’. She is currently working on her first cookbook, an as-yet-untitled collection of essential Nigerian Recipes (Ten Speed Press, Fall 2021), rooted in the converging cuisines of her hometown Lagos.
